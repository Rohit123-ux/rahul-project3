🛡️ HealthGuard — Multi Disease Risk Assessment System

A full-stack web application that predicts the risk of Heart Disease, Diabetes, and Kidney Disease using an ensemble of scikit-learn ML models. Features a patient portal with risk trend charts, downloadable PDF health reports, and automatic risk-increase alerts, alongside a doctor dashboard for monitoring all registered patients. Built entirely from scratch as a B.Tech Final Year Project.
